REPORTS FROM THE GHANA HEALTH SERVICE INDICATE THAT THE CURRENT CHOLERA OUTBREAK STARTED IN SEPTEMBER LAST YEAR IN THE CENTRAL REGION AND IT’S KEPT SPREADING AND KILLING PEOPLE. FIVE REGIONS ARE CURRENTLY INFECTED AND THEY INCLUDE, GREATER ACCRA, CENTRAL, EASTERN, UPPER WEST AND THE NORTHERN REGIONS. THE REPORTED INFECTED CASE ARE ABOUT 5,308 AND THESE FIGURES ARE RISING BY THE DAY, A TOTAL OF 67 DEATHS HAS SINCE BEEN REPORT GIVING A DEATH RATE OF 1.5%.
