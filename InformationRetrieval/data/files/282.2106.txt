 The most recent Ebola outbreak began in March 2014 and, as of Oct. 5, 2014, there have been 8,033 reported Ebola cases in 2014, according to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC). Of these cases, 4,461 have been confirmed by laboratory testing. The total number of deaths from Ebola in 2014 is 3,865; the current fatality rate is 50 percent.
