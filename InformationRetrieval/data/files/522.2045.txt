30 December 2014 -- 2014 saw an unprecedented outbreak of the lethal infectious disease, Ebola virus disease, that caused over 7,500 deaths and untold suffering in West Africa. It also saw examples of great human courage, resourcefulness and partnership as people in the affected countries and other countries around the globe raced to respond to this greatest challenge to the health and wellbeing of all humanity.
