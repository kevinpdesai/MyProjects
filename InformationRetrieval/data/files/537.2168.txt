Management
Management typically requires aggressive supportive care focused on hemodynamic support, blood transfusion, reversal of coagulopathy, correction of electrolyte disturbances, and treatment of any secondary infections.
No specific antiviral treatment is available.
Investigational approaches used in the treatment of Ebola patients include:
transfusion of convalescent blood or sera
ZMapp, a cocktail of monoclonal antibodies targeting Ebola virus antigens
