In a Gallup poll of over 1,000 Americans, one fifth were worried about contracting Ebola. Still, the chances you'll be exposed to Ebola in the United States are very low because of the tight infection-control practices of public health workers here.CDC workers are taking steps to identify anyone who may have been in contact with the U.S. patients with Ebola. Health workers are stepping up infection-control practices that will include isolating any of the patients' contacts who may become ill.
