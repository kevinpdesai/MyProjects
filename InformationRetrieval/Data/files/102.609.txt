Blood transfusions from an Ebola survivor are endorsed as a priority treatment by the World Health Organization. Patients who survive Ebola develop antibodies that can last for a decade, according to the CDC. They bind to the virus and prevent it from multiplying. Brantly has donated the plasma in his blood to several patients, including Pham, Mukpo and Sacra, all of whom survived. Writebol donated plasma to Spencer on Friday.
