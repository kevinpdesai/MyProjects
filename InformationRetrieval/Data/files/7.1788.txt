Uncomplicated typhoid feverEmpirical treatment if infection is likely to have originated in Asia - azithromycin.Multiple-resistant - fluoroquinolone (5-7 days), or cefixime (7-14 days).Quinolone-resistant - azithromycin (7 days) or ceftriaxone (10-14 days).If the infection is likely to be from Africa, South America or Central America, fluoroquinolones (eg, ciprofloxacin) may still be used (5-7 days). Fewer than 4% of infections imported to the UK from Africa are resistant to fluoroquinolones.
